## Benefit Bullets

1. **Instant social previews** — See exactly how your pages render on Facebook/LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and WhatsApp/Slack. No more guessing whether your OG tags work.

3. **Edit, export, and ship** — Edit meta tags right in the browser with character counters, export full reports as JSON or CSV, and save printable HTML audits for your team.
